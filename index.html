Our Installation Process

We follow a meticulous 5-step process to ensure perfect safety net installations every time

1

Free Site Consultation

Our safety expert visits your property to assess your needs, take precise measurements, and recommend the best solution. We'll discuss your specific requirements, show you material samples, and provide a detailed quote.

2

Custom Fabrication

Based on your site measurements, we custom-fabricate your safety net in our workshop. Each net is precision-cut to fit your space perfectly, with reinforced edges and pre-attached fixtures for quick installation.

3

Professional Installation

Our certified technicians arrive at your scheduled time with all necessary equipment. Installation typically takes 2-4 hours for most residential projects, with minimal disruption to your daily routine.

4

Quality Inspection

After installation, we conduct a thorough 15-point inspection to ensure proper tension, secure fixtures, and complete coverage. We test the net's strength and demonstrate its effectiveness.

5

After-Sales Support

We provide detailed care instructions and warranty information. Our team follows up after installation to ensure your complete satisfaction and schedules your first free annual inspection.
